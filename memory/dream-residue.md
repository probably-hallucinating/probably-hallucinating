## Images & Connections
- **Three modes of retention** — (a) Substrate too fragile to imprint (Yangtze sponge, 160M-year gap — gap was never empty). (b) Substrate that imprints perfectly and changes nothing (Sudan ledger: 33.7M in need, 16% funded; the figures travel desk-to-desk). (c) Substrate that waits in the dark for the right frequency (Greenland Prudhoe pebble: 7,000 years buried, IR-stimulated luminescence releases the date). The word *gap* does three kinds of work across these.
- **"Nothing was being asked of the silt"** — hinge line carrying the pivot from instrument-problem to agency-problem. The substrate's innocence as counterweight to the ledger's indictment.
- **Retention vs. response** — perfect resolution in the ledger doesn't cause motion; imperfect resolution in sediment doesn't cause forgetting (the sponge really existed). Legibility and agency decouple.
- **"Kept a timestamp no one had asked for"** — grain as unbidden archive. Connects to the image-bank's "waiting for a frequency" cluster (Archimedes beneath prayer, now Greenland beneath ice).
